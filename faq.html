Frequently Asked Questions

How can I purchase tickets?

Tickets can be purchased through our online website. A debit card or credit card is required for purchase. Please click "Tickets" in the header for more information.

What is your return policy for tickets?

All tickets sales are final sale and cannot be returned or refunded. You are eligible to resell tickets at your own risk.

What is the most popular package?

Our most popular package is Package C (VIP Package). This package costs $500 and includes VIP seats, VIP Parking, and access to unlimited food and drinks for the duration of the festival.

Are there any food options inside the music festival?

There are a number of food options availible. The entire list of options can be seen on our homepage. You can navigate to our homepage by clicking "Home" at the top left of your screen.

Where can I find a restroom at the festival?

Once you arrive to Bouncin' in da' Bay, there will be restrooms scattered throughout the fairgrounds. If you need help finding one, feel free to ask one of our representatives on site.

Are masks required at Bouncin' in da' Bay?

While masks are not required, they are highly recommended. Santa Clara county recommends all unvaxinated individuals to wear a mask when not actively drinking or eating.

Can we bring in our own food and drinks?

Unfortunately you are not allowed to bring in your own food and drink. There will be plenty of food and drink availible for purchase once you enter the fairgrounds.

Is smoking allowed in the festival?

Smoking legal substances will be allowed only in the designated areas. Smoking outside of the designated areas may result in getting kicked out without refund.
